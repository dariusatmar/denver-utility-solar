# The Case for Utility-Scale Solar + Battery in Denver

A data-driven analysis of why the City of Denver should invest in utility-scale solar photovoltaics and battery storage.

## What This Is

An independent analytical piece that examines:
- Denver's solar irradiance resource (NREL data)
- Utility-scale LCOE vs. Colorado's current generation costs (Lazard, EIA)
- Battery storage economics and Handmer's utilization framework
- Land requirements and site options (DIA, eastern plains, brownfields)
- Colorado's policy framework and procurement options
- The data center opportunity

## Data Sources

- NREL Solar Resource API & Annual Technology Baseline
- EIA Colorado Electricity Profile 2024
- Lazard LCOE+ 2025
- SEIA Colorado Solar Report
- Xcel Energy Clean Energy Plan
- Denver Office of Climate Action
